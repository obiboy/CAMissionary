In response to this urgent need for medical assistance, CAM opened a mini-clinic in Nnarambia, Ahiara, Ahiazu Mbaise where individuals within and outside the community can check their blood pressure and glucose (sugar level) for free. This clinic is open 3 days a week and staffed by three graduate level qualified nurses. The clinic was opened by His Royal Highness, Eze, Dr. Pious C. Onyekwere, JP(Royal) MSI and Rev. Fr. Kyrian Chilaka in March 25, 2022. In addition to checking blood pressure and glucose level, the clinic also provides palliatives such vitamins, pain medicine, treating and dressing minor cuts and bruises and simple medical advice.
