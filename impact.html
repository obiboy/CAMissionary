Past Missions

Medical Mission 2018

March 2018

Nnarambia Ahiara Mbaise, Imo State, Nigeria

In March 2018, with the help of the good people of St. Elizabeth Catholic Church in Pflugerville and the local leaders, Compassionate Angels Missionary was able to provide healthcare services to 800 people in Nnarambia. We organized a seamless three day medical mission which provided doctors' consultations and diagnoses as well as lab work and medicine.
